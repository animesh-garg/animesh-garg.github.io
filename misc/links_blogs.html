List of some interesting acedemic blogs.

  • Computer vision
    • Quantombone
      “The philosophy of computational object recognition, scene understanding, machine learning, and musings on the future of computer vision” from Tomasz Malisiewicz at CMU.
    • Computer Blindness
      The research blog of Roman Shapovalov at the Moscow State University.
    • Learning in Vision
      Vidit Jain’s (Yahoo! Labs) blog on computer vision, with a focus on information retrieval.
  • General machine learning
    • Please Scoop Me
      Jonathan Chang has good posts about topic models and random statistical visualizations.
    • Inductio Ex Machine
      Mark Reid’s research blog on machine learning and statistical inference.
    • Inherent Uncertainty
      Jake Abernethy (and some other mostly Berkeley ML people) post “a range of thoughts on Learning and Game-playing, Prediction and Decision-making.”
    • Hunch: Machine Learning Theory
      John Langford’s blog.
  • Robotics
    • Robot Zeitgeist
      This is a general news blog covering Robotics, AI, Machine Learning and Computer Vision.
    • SurgRob
      This is a news blog listing info on developments in medical and surgical robotics.
  • Readings for students in AI

A number of these links are courtsey Sergey Karayev
